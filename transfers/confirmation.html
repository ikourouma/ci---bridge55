Frequently Asked Questions
How do I modify or cancel my transfer booking?

You can modify or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before pickup through your Bridge55 account or by contacting our support team. Cancellations made within 24 hours may incur a fee.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We track your flight automatically. If your flight is delayed, we'll adjust your pickup time accordingly. Your driver will wait up to 60 minutes after your flight lands at no extra cost.

How will I find my driver at the airport?

Your driver will be waiting in the arrivals hall holding a sign with your name. You'll receive the driver's name and phone number via SMS 24 hours before pickup. You can also call or WhatsApp them directly.

Can I request a child seat?

Yes! Child seats can be requested during booking or by contacting the provider directly. Most providers offer infant, toddler, and booster seats at no additional charge when requested in advance.

What if I have more luggage than expected?

If you have additional luggage, please contact your transfer provider as soon as possible. They may be able to accommodate extra bags or arrange a larger vehicle for a small additional fee.

Is the price fixed or can it change?

The price shown at booking is the final price you pay. Unlike taxis, there are no surge pricing or hidden fees. The fare covers your journey, meet & greet service, and 60 minutes of free waiting time.
