Booking & Reservations

How do I make a booking on Bridge55?

Making a booking on Bridge55 is simple:

  • Go to our Search Hub or select a service from the menu
  • Enter your travel details (dates, destinations, travelers)
  • Browse and compare options
  • Select your preferred option and click "Book Now"
  • Complete payment using your preferred method
  • Receive instant confirmation via email and SMS

You can also use Dia, our AI concierge, to help you find and book the perfect trip!

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, most bookings can be modified depending on the service provider's policy. To modify your booking:

  • Go to My Trips
  • Find your booking and click "Manage"
  • Select "Modify Booking"
  • Make your changes and confirm

Note: Some modifications may incur additional fees depending on the provider's policy.

I didn't receive my booking confirmation. What should I do?

If you haven't received your confirmation:

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Verify the email address on your account is correct
  • Log in to My Trips to view your booking
  • Contact our 24/7 support for assistance

How do I book for a group?

For groups of 10 or more travelers, we offer special group rates:

  • Contact our group booking team
  • Provide your travel dates, destinations, and group size
  • Receive a customized quote within 24 hours

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

Bridge55 supports multiple payment options tailored for Africa:

  • Bridge Wallet: Preloaded wallet for faster checkout
  • Mobile Money: M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Orange Money
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, Verve
  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank transfers (local & international)

How do I apply a promo code?

To apply a promo code:

  • Complete your booking selection
  • On the payment page, find the "Promo Code" field
  • Enter your code and click "Apply"
  • The discount will be reflected in your total

Tip: Ask Dia for current promotions and deals!

What currency are prices displayed in?

Prices are displayed in USD by default but can be converted to your local currency. We support KES, NGN, ZAR, GHS, EGP, and more. Currency conversion is based on real-time exchange rates.

How long do refunds take?

Refund processing times vary by payment method:

  • Bridge Wallet: Instant
  • Mobile Money: 1-3 business days
  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 5-14 business days

Cancellations & Refunds

How do I cancel my booking?

To cancel a booking:

  • Go to My Trips
  • Find the booking you want to cancel
  • Click "Cancel Booking"
  • Review the cancellation policy and confirm

Note: Cancellation fees may apply depending on the provider's policy and timing.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by service type and provider:

  • Flights: Depends on airline policy (typically 24-hour free cancellation)
  • Hotels: Look for "Free Cancellation" badges
  • Experiences: Usually 48-72 hours before activity
  • Transfers: Usually 24 hours before pickup

Always check the specific cancellation terms on your booking confirmation.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is quick and free:

  • Click "Sign In" in the top navigation
  • Select "Create Account"
  • Enter your email, phone, and password
  • Verify your email or phone
  • Start earning rewards!

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

To reset your password:

  • Click "Sign In" then "Forgot Password"
  • Enter your registered email or phone
  • Check your inbox for the reset link
  • Create a new strong password

Rewards & Loyalty

How do I earn Bridge Points?

Earn points on every booking:

  • Flights: 2 points per $1 spent
  • Hotels: 3 points per $1 spent
  • Experiences: 2 points per $1 spent
  • Referrals: 500 points per friend who books

Higher tiers earn multiplied points!

How do I redeem my points?

Points can be redeemed at checkout:

  • 100 points = $1 discount
  • Apply points on the payment page
  • Or convert to Bridge Wallet credit

What are the membership tiers?

Bridge55 has 4 membership tiers:

  • Explorer: 0-999 points (1x earning)
  • Voyager: 1,000-4,999 points (1.5x earning)
  • Pioneer: 5,000-14,999 points (2x earning)
  • Legend: 15,000+ points (3x earning + VIP perks)
