Frequently Asked Questions
How old do I need to be to fly with Kenya Airways?
Unaccompanied minors (ages 5-11) can travel alone with our Unaccompanied Minor service. Children 12-15 can travel alone without special assistance, though the service is available if requested. Adults must be 18 or older.
What documents do I need for this flight?
You'll need a valid passport (with at least 6 months validity), visa for Ghana (if required - check eVisa eligibility), and proof of yellow fever vaccination. COVID-19 vaccination certificate may be required depending on current regulations.
Can I change or cancel this booking?
Yes! Economy Class tickets can be changed for a fee of $75 plus any fare difference. Cancellations receive a refund minus $100. Free changes and cancellations are available within 24 hours of booking. Business Class offers more flexibility.
Is travel insurance included?
Basic travel insurance is not included in the ticket price but can be added during booking for $25 per person. We highly recommend insurance for international flights, covering trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and lost baggage.
Can I select my seat in advance?
Yes! Standard seat selection is free during check-in (online or at airport). Premium seats (extra legroom, exit rows) can be selected for $30-50 at booking or anytime before check-in. Flying Blue members get free advanced seat selection.
What meals are served on this flight?
A complimentary hot meal is served approximately 1 hour after takeoff, with options including African and international cuisine. Beverages (including wine and beer) are complimentary throughout the flight. Special meals (vegetarian, halal, etc.) can be requested 24 hours before departure.

  • Check-in opens 3 hours before departure
