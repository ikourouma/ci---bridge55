Frequently Asked Questions
How do I check in for my flight online?
Online check-in opens 24 hours before your scheduled departure. Visit the Kenya Airways website or use their mobile app to check in. You'll need your booking reference (PNR) and passport details. After check-in, you can download your boarding pass or have it sent to your email.
Can I change or cancel my flight booking?
Changes are permitted with a fee of $75 plus any fare difference. This ticket is non-refundable, but you may be able to receive credit for future travel depending on the fare rules. Contact our support team or the airline directly to discuss your options.
What is the baggage allowance for my flight?
Your Economy class ticket includes: 1 carry-on bag (max 7kg, 56√ó36√ó23cm) and 1 checked bag (max 23kg, 158cm total dimensions). Additional or overweight baggage fees apply: 2nd bag $40, overweight (23-32kg) $50, oversized (159-203cm) $50.
How early should I arrive at the airport?
For international flights, arrive at least 3 hours before departure. Check-in counters close 60 minutes before departure. This allows time for check-in, security screening, immigration, and reaching your gate. Boarding typically begins 30-45 minutes before departure.
Do I need a visa for Uganda?
Most nationalities require a visa to enter Uganda. You can apply for an e-Visa online at visas.immigration.go.ug. Processing typically takes 2-3 business days. East African Community citizens (Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi, South Sudan) may have different requirements.
What if my flight is delayed or cancelled?
In case of delays or cancellations, the airline will notify you via email/SMS. Depending on the situation, you may be entitled to rebooking, refund, or compensation. Contact the airline directly for immediate assistance. Bridge55 support is also available 24/7 to help coordinate.
