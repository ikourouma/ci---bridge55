Frequently Asked Questions
How do I modify or cancel my booking?
You can modify or cancel your booking for free until December 13, 2025. Click the "Modify Booking" button above or contact our support team at support@bridge55.com. After the free cancellation period, the first night will be non-refundable.
What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in time is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability. Contact the hotel directly to arrange.
Is airport transfer included?
Airport transfer is not included in the room rate but can be arranged for an additional fee. The hotel is located approximately 25 minutes from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport. Contact the hotel at least 24 hours before arrival to arrange pickup.
What is included in the breakfast?
Your booking includes a full breakfast buffet served daily from 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM. The buffet features a wide selection of continental and African dishes, fresh fruits, pastries, hot items, and made-to-order eggs.
How can I request special arrangements?
For special arrangements such as room decorations, flowers, champagne, or surprise celebrations, please contact the hotel directly at reservations@hemingways.co.ke or call +254 20 327 4100 at least 48 hours before your arrival.
What is the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation is available until December 13, 2025 (48 hours before check-in). Cancellations made after this date will incur a charge equivalent to the first night's stay. No-shows will be charged the full booking amount.
