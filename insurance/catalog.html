Frequently Asked Questions

When does my coverage start?
Your coverage starts at midnight on your selected trip start date. For trip cancellation coverage, it begins immediately after purchase. We recommend purchasing insurance at the same time as booking your trip.
Can I extend my coverage while traveling?
Yes! You can extend your coverage through the Bridge55 app or by contacting our 24/7 support team. Extensions are subject to availability and must be requested before your current coverage expires.
How do I file a claim?
Filing a claim is easy! Use the BridgeCare section in your Bridge55 app to submit claims with photos of receipts and documents. Most claims are processed within 5-7 business days. For emergencies, call our 24/7 hotline for immediate assistance.
Are pre-existing conditions covered?
Pre-existing conditions are covered under our Elite plan only. For Essential and Voyager plans, pre-existing conditions are excluded. If you have ongoing health conditions, we recommend the Elite plan for comprehensive coverage.
