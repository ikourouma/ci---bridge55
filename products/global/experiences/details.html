About This Experience

Embark on an unforgettable 3-day safari adventure in Kenya's world-renowned Masai Mara National Reserve. Home to the famous Great Migration and the Big Five, the Masai Mara offers one of Africa's most spectacular wildlife viewing experiences.

Stay in luxury tented camps under the African stars, enjoy gourmet bush dining, and witness breathtaking sunrises over the savannah. Our experienced Maasai guides will share their deep knowledge of the land and its magnificent wildlife.

Itinerary

D1
Nairobi to Masai Mara

Depart Nairobi early morning for the scenic drive to Masai Mara. Stop at the Great Rift Valley viewpoint. Arrive at the luxury camp for lunch, followed by an afternoon game drive. Sundowner drinks in the bush and dinner under the stars.

D2
Full Day Game Drives

Early morning sunrise game drive to catch predators in action. Return for breakfast at camp. Optional Maasai village visit. Afternoon game drive focusing on the Big Five. Evening campfire with bush dinner.

D3
Morning Safari & Return

Final early morning game drive. Return to camp for breakfast and checkout. Scenic drive back to Nairobi, arriving by late afternoon. Transfer to your hotel or JKIA.
