Frequently Asked Questions
How do I modify or cancel my car rental booking?
You can modify or cancel your booking for free up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Click the "Modify Booking" button above or contact our support team at cars@bridge55.com. Cancellations made within 24 hours of pickup may incur a fee equivalent to one day's rental.
What documents do I need to pick up the car?
You'll need to present: (1) A valid driver's license held for at least 1 year, (2) A valid passport or national ID, (3) A credit card in the main driver's name for the security deposit. International drivers may need an International Driving Permit (IDP) depending on their country of origin.
What is the fuel policy?
The vehicle will be provided with a full tank of fuel. You are expected to return it with a full tank. If the car is returned with less fuel, a refueling charge plus a service fee will be applied. We recommend refueling at a gas station near the return location before drop-off.
What does the insurance cover?
Your booking includes Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) and Theft Protection (TP). This covers damage to or theft of the vehicle with an excess/deductible amount. You can reduce or eliminate the excess by purchasing additional coverage at pickup. Personal belongings are not covered - we recommend travel insurance for this.
Can I add an additional driver?
Yes, additional drivers can be added at the rental counter. Each additional driver must present a valid driver's license and meet the age requirements. A daily fee of approximately $5-15 per additional driver may apply. Some loyalty programs include free additional drivers - check at the counter.
What happens if I return the car late?
Most rental companies offer a grace period of 30-60 minutes. After that, you may be charged for an additional day or hourly fees. If you know you'll be late, contact the rental company in advance to extend your rental and avoid higher walk-in rates.
